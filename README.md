# Family Capacity Planner
Simple yet useful tool for families to plan their work- and kindergarden hours.
If you have ever needed to design wekly work hours or a recurring schedule for your family or community this is for you.
This visualisation tool fosters your discussing about your opportunities and lets you design them colaboratively.

# Usage
- Visit [https://bastianowicz.github.io/family-capacity-planner/]([url](https://bastianowicz.github.io/family-capacity-planner/))
- Define your family and their constraints
- Drag the bars of each family member in the week planner to change their occupied hours
- Click on empty spots to add another occupied block (to design breaks)
- Click save to reuse your design
- Click load to reinitiate your saved setup
